STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

1. Never share your OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone.
   
2. Always verify payment requests before approving them.
   
3. Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers.
   
4. Use only official and trusted payment apps.
   
5. Enable SMS and app notifications for transactions.
 
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
    
7. Keep your mobile apps and phone updated regularly.
  
8. Immediately report any suspicious activity.

UPI/Financial Safety Tips:

9. Remember: You do NOT need to enter PIN to receive money.

10. Double-check the receiver’s details before sending money.
